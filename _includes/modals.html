Crowdsourcing

The process of obtaining needed services, ideas or content by soliciting contributions from a large group of people, and specially from an online community.

Collective micro research

Similar to crowdfunding or micro financing. Providing time or intellectual resources instead of financial. Resarch projects can benefit from collective cooperation adding all the small contributions of time of each volunteer: 1000 volunteers x 1 minute = 1000 extra minutes for research.

Citizen science

(Crowd science, crowd-sourced science, civic science, volunteer monitoring or networked science) is scientific research conducted, in whole or in part, by amateur or nonprofessional scientists. Citizen science is sometimes included in terms such as "public participation in scientific research".

Collective intelligence

Shared or group intelligence that emerges from teh collaboration and collective efforts of many individuals. The use of the Internet promtes online interaction between users resulting in a form of collective intelligence.
