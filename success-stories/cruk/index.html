How crowdsourcing

is helping to beat the cancer

Trailblazer is Cancer Research UK's PYBOSSA-powered tool.

It has allowed thousands of people worldwide to help

scientists spot cancer cells and accelerate research with 90%

accuracy, after only 25 minutes of training.

scroll down

Starting point

Trailblazer aimed to find wheter citizen science could be a

realiable method to accelerate cancer research

Citizen science holds true potential where researchers want to identify trends and patterns in large volumes of data.
This study investigates the efficacy of various training methods to improve accuracy on detecting several cancer type cells.
To do so, enhanced training and more feedback was provided and interactive tutorials and an easy-to-use interface were used to get people doing quality analysis, fast.
